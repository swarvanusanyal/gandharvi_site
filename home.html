Indian classical dates back to at least 1000 BC in the rich and diverse Indian history. From time immemoroable, Music was integral part of Indian tradition and spirituality as according to Indian philosophy, this experiential universe is made from pure thought which was manifested through primal Sound (Shabda). In SamaVeda, particular emphasis has been given on how to chant the hymns. From there on different cultures and civilization has enriched and shaped Indian classical music. Moumita, the founder and instructor of music school Gandharvi is practicing, researching and performing Indian classical and semi-classical songs in different concerts for last 20 years.
